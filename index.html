Agenda Reader

Drop in a council meeting agenda PDF. You get a readable summary of the business items and a spreadsheet matching the council_papers layout — built entirely in your browser.

🔒 Stays on your machine. The PDF is read locally; nothing is uploaded.
Reads the PDF text layer with pdf.js and writes the workbook with SheetJS, both from a CDN. Scanned image-only PDFs can't be read. An independent community tool — not affiliated with or endorsed by any council.
